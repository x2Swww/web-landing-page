Synopsis of Your Name

A boy and a girl the same age mysteriously switch bodies while sleeping. Mitsuha, a countryside girl dreaming of Tokyo, and Taki, a Tokyo student working part-time, share this strange experience. Though memories fade when they return to their own bodies, their search for answers brings them closer and forges a deep bond.

Taki Tachibana

Taki Tachibana a high school boy in Tokyo, enjoys life with his friends. He works part-time at an Italian restaurant and has everything in life.

Mitsuha Miyamizu

Mitsuha Miyamizu is an ordinary high school girl living in the countryside of Hitomi City. She dreams of going to Tokyo because she is bored with her usual life and city. She wants to experience life in the capital city, which might make her happier.
